# Interest Rates — Frequently Asked Questions

## What is the current interest rate?

Our rates are floating, linked to the repo rate. The current base rate is **8.75% per annum**.

Your actual rate = Base Rate + Credit Score Modifier + DTI Modifier + Risk Modifier − Fast-Track Discount (if applicable)

## How are rate modifiers applied?

**Credit score modifier**
- Excellent (800+): −0.50%
- Good (700–799): 0.00%
- Fair (650–699): +0.75%
- Poor/fast-track (600–649): +1.50%

**DTI modifier**
- DTI 0–35% (Comfortable): −0.25%
- DTI 35–45% (Moderate): 0.00%
- DTI 45–55% (Stretched): +0.50%

**Risk band modifier**
- Low: 0.00%
- Medium: +0.50%
- High: +1.25%

**Employment type modifier**
- Salaried: 0.00%
- Self-employed: +0.50%
- Business owner: +0.75%
- Retired: +0.25%

**Fast-track discount (tied property):** −0.25%

---

## Fixed vs Floating — which is better?

We currently offer only **floating rate** loans linked to the external benchmark (RBI repo rate). This means:
- Your EMI may change if the repo rate changes
- You benefit when rates fall
- Rate resets happen every quarter

Fixed rate products are under review and may be launched in a future product cycle.

## Will my rate change after disbursement?

Your rate is floating and will be revised if the RBI repo rate changes. You will be notified at least 30 days before any rate change takes effect. You may choose to:
- Keep the same EMI and adjust the tenure
- Keep the same tenure and adjust the EMI

---

## Is there a penalty for early repayment?

- **Home Loan and Top-Up Loan:** No prepayment penalty (RBI mandate for floating rate loans)
- **Loan Against Property:** 2% of outstanding principal if prepaid within the first 12 months; nil thereafter
